# IT Security Policy

## 1. Password Requirements
- Minimum length: 12 characters
- Must include uppercase, lowercase, a number, and a special character
- Passwords must be changed every 90 days
- Reuse of the last 5 passwords is not allowed

## 2. Device Usage
All company laptops must have full-disk encryption enabled and an approved antivirus
solution installed before being connected to the corporate network. Personal devices
accessing company email must be enrolled in the Mobile Device Management (MDM) system.

## 3. Data Classification
Company data is classified into four levels:
1. **Public** — freely shareable
2. **Internal** — for employees only
3. **Confidential** — restricted to specific teams
4. **Restricted** — highest sensitivity (e.g., financial records, PII)

Confidential and Restricted data must never be sent over unencrypted channels or stored
on personal cloud drives.

## 4. Incident Reporting
Any suspected security incident (phishing email, lost device, unauthorized access) must
be reported to the IT Security team within 1 hour of discovery via the incident hotline
or the internal ticketing system.

## 5. Access Reviews
Access permissions for all critical systems are reviewed quarterly. Access not used for
90 consecutive days is automatically revoked and must be re-requested through the access
management portal.

## 6. Remote Access
VPN is mandatory for accessing internal systems from outside the office network.
Multi-factor authentication (MFA) is required for all VPN and email logins.
