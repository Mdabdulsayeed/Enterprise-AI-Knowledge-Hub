COMPANY LEAVE POLICY
====================

1. Annual Leave
Every full-time employee is entitled to 24 days of paid annual leave per calendar year.
Leave accrues at the rate of 2 days per month of service. Unused leave, up to a maximum
of 10 days, may be carried forward to the next calendar year. Any leave beyond this cap
is forfeited unless approved otherwise by HR.

2. Sick Leave
Employees are entitled to 12 days of paid sick leave per year. A medical certificate is
required for sick leave exceeding 2 consecutive days. Sick leave does not carry forward
and resets at the start of each calendar year.

3. Maternity and Paternity Leave
Female employees are entitled to 26 weeks of paid maternity leave. Male employees are
entitled to 2 weeks of paid paternity leave, to be availed within 3 months of the child's
birth.

4. Work From Home Policy
Employees may work from home up to 2 days per week, subject to manager approval.
Requests must be submitted at least 24 hours in advance through the HR portal.

5. Leave Application Process
All leave requests must be submitted through the internal HR portal at least 3 working
days in advance, except in emergencies. Approval is granted by the reporting manager.
Unapproved absences are recorded as Leave Without Pay (LWP).

6. Public Holidays
The company observes 12 public holidays per year as per the regional holiday calendar,
published every January on the HR portal.
