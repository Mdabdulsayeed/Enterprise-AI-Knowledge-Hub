CloudSuite Product FAQ

What is CloudSuite?

CloudSuite is an enterprise project management and collaboration platform that combines task tracking, document sharing, and team messaging into a single dashboard. It is available as a web app and as native desktop and mobile apps.

What are the pricing tiers?

CloudSuite offers three tiers: Starter (up to 10 users, $8/user/month), Business (up to 100 users, $15/user/month), and Enterprise (unlimited users, custom pricing with dedicated support and single sign-on).

Does CloudSuite support integrations?

Yes. CloudSuite integrates natively with Slack, Microsoft Teams, Google Workspace, Jira, and Salesforce. Additional integrations are available through the CloudSuite API and a public webhook system.

What is the data retention policy?

Deleted projects are retained in a recovery bin for 30 days before permanent deletion. Enterprise customers can request custom retention periods of up to 7 years for compliance purposes.

Is CloudSuite compliant with data protection regulations?

CloudSuite is SOC 2 Type II certified and GDPR compliant. Data residency options (US, EU, and APAC regions) are available for Enterprise customers.

How do I get support?

Starter and Business customers can reach support via email with a target response time of 24 hours. Enterprise customers receive 24/7 priority support with a dedicated account manager and a 1-hour response SLA for critical issues.
